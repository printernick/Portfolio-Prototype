Summer iD Tech

July 2019 - August 2019

Summer iD tech is a STEM summer program that teaches young students tech curriculum. My role was a tutor where I had to prepare and teach a week long course. I also had to manage and facilitate these students to adhere to camp guidelines. I worked alongside a team of other talented instructors to cultivate a fun and educational environment. Some courses I taught include: "Assemble Your Take-Home Laptop and Code in Python," "Cybersecurity and Encryption," and "Python Coding and AI with Take-Home Cozmo."

Outreach Coordinator

June 2017 - June 2018

The Campus Village housing community hired a small board to organize successful, creative programs and events for the community. One event in particular was designing our very own escape room for residents. We had puzzles and hints around our community center and I thought it would be an interesting idea to have a small text-based game as one of the puzzles to escaping. I coded it in Python and utilized classes and inheritance to represent rooms and items within the game.
